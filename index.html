All eyes on

Hidilyn Diaz


–

The first female, the first weightlifter, and the first Mindanao-based athlete to enter the Olympic podium

Hidilyn Diaz brought glory to the country after ending a 20-year medal drought in the Olympics. She had represented the country in the Olympics in 2008 and 2012, before finally breaking through with a silver medal in Rio de Janeiro.

She also became the first medal winner from the Philippines since boxer Onyok Velasco's silver medal conquest in the 1996 edition in Atlanta. This is also the first time a medal was won for the Philippines outside of boxing since 1936.
